Come to improve your teamplay?

If you're coming to this website in interest of my services, chances are high that you are part of a team that's either working on breaking into the collegiate scene or wants to compete within the Open Division. If so, then you have come to the right place. Whether you're wanting to improve team coordination, individual player skills, or aren't even sure where you need to start, I am here to guide you every step of the way.


Overwatch is a team-based multiplayer first-person shooter in which two teams of six go head to head. Each player selects a “hero” from a roster of over 30 characters, each of which has unique skill sets and abilities that fit within a general team role: tank, damage, or support. When looking to improve as a team, you must ask yourself how each player contributes to the team's cohesion as a whole, and what improvements are required.
