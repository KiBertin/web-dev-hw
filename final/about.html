About Me

Kian "keydei" Bertin

Hello there! I am keydei, a longtime player and coach for Overwatch. I have over five years of experience of teamleading and coaching, with my most notable role being the head coach of the University of Montana's varsity and junior varsity Overwatch teams.

During my time as a coach, I have seen my players go from being unskilled and uncoordinated in a team setting to becoming the closest of friends and working together without prompting, taking down teams several degrees higher in individual skill through sheer synergy.

My primary strengths as a coach is understanding of teamfight dynamics, positioning, team communication, and thorough understanding of what makes my players tick. By understanding my players, I can more effectively guide them maximize their greatest strengths and have their teammates cover their weaknesses without issue.

Have you been finding your team in a rut where you know that you have the potential to improve, but aren't sure where to start? I am completly confident that my services will help push you to the next level. Send me a message to book an appointment today!
